Left Hand Keys – Touch Typing Lesson

1 minute read

 

This is the touch typing lesson for left hand. All words will be on left hand only.

When do you need this lesson?

Some people play the game and surfing Internet so much, and then they use only on left hand on keyboard and right hand use the mouse. The most key is press by left hand. For the gamer, they use left hand to convenient move the player character. Therefore, most of the time they use WASD keys for moving, they are not aware of that slowly becomes their habits and it’s hard to change.

 

Or someone wants to improve the accuracy on the left hand, typing proper on the left hand.

How to touch typing?

The starting position for your fingers is called the home row. This is where your fingers live.

The bumps or nipples found on the F and J keys on the keyboard helps you correctly position their left and right hand on the keyboard without having to look at the keyboard.

Every word in this lesson is just located on the left hand only.

The space bar is pressed with either thumb. Most people probably use only one thumb, the one on their dominant hand as long as you’re comfortable with it.

Some advice

The lesson to strengthen left hand and increase the speed and accuracy on the left hand. Just focus on the small group of keys to remember key’s location and when you are good at this. You can combine the previous lesson to type the whole text with both hand without looking at the keyboard.

Each test should be less 200 words, just take short practice each time, and remember that then main purpose here to remember the keys location. Try make speed over 30 wpm.

If you’re boring this lesson, you can use your own text to practice, or skip this if you want to. But I recommend that, you should put a little effort to practice this if you left hand is typing inaccuracy or improperly touch typing!

 

Hope that helps you better!
